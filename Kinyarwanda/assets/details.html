Ibisobanuro by'inguzanyo

Iyi nzu nziza y'umuryango iri mu karere ka Rwamagana, Muyumbu. Ifite amazu 3 y'ibyumba, amazu 2 y'ibyumba, n'ubuso bw'ubutaka bwa 1,500 sqft. Iyi nzu iri hirya no hino mu butaka bw'ubwiza kandi ifite icyerekezo cyiza cy'ubutaka bw'ahantu.

Ibindi bintu bifite:

  • Icyumba cy'ibiryo gikoresha ibikoresho by'ikirenga
  • Icyumba cy'ibiryo kinini
  • Ubutaka bunini bufite urubura
  • Umuryango w'inzira uhagije
