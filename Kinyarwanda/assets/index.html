Ibyerekeye

Iyi weburayisi niyo gusa kugirango ubashe kumenya amakuru. Amazu yose yanditse ari leaseholds, kuko u Rwanda buri wese afite ububasha bwo kugenga isi. Leaseholds birashobora kuba agaciro kanini, ariko nyamuneka ibikorwa byose bikorwa binyuze mu nzira zemewe. Ntitwari dufite inshingano z'ibikorwa by'uburyarya bikorwa nabandi.

Icyitonderwa: Iyi weburayisi ntishyura. Niba ushaka gutangaza inzu cyangwa kugura leasehold, nyamuneka koresha iforumu yo kwandikira kugirango utugezeho ibibazo. Turi kumwe kugufasha!

Inama nziza: Buri gihe menya neza ko amazu cyangwa itangazo ari by'ukuri mbere yo kwishyura. Nibyo byose biba bisanzwe ariko bitagoranye, birashobora kuba ari ukuryarya.
